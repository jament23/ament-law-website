Areas We Serve

Your local attorneys for Westmoreland County and beyond.

Serving All of Westmoreland County

Our firm is proud to be based in Murrysville, but our roots run deep throughout Westmoreland County. We are committed to providing accessible, high-quality legal services to all our neighbors.

While we serve the entire county, we have significant experience helping clients in the following communities:

Murrysville, PA (Our Home Base)

As a proud Murrysville-based law firm, we are deeply invested in our local community. We provide our neighbors with convenient, trusted, and expert legal counsel for all their estate, real estate, and business needs.

Greensburg, PA

As the county seat, Greensburg is a hub of activity. We frequently assist Greensburg clients with real estate closings, business formation, and probate matters in the Westmoreland County Courthouse.

Monroeville, PA

As a major commercial and residential hub, we are perfectly positioned to serve Monroeville clients. We provide experienced counsel for business law and real estate transactions, as well as comprehensive estate planning for Monroeville families.

Plum, PA

Bordering us to the west, Plum is a community we know well. We assist many Plum residents with buying and selling homes, setting up their first will, and planning for their family's future.

Harrison City, PA

Serving the greater Penn-Trafford area, including Harrison City, is a core part of our practice. We offer accessible and practical legal advice for estate administration, real estate, and elder law needs.

Latrobe, PA

From family businesses to estate planning, our firm understands the legal needs of the Latrobe community. We help Latrobe families protect their assets and plan for the future.

Ligonier, PA

We are proud to offer our full range of legal services to the residents and business owners of Ligonier. From our Murrysville office, we provide trusted counsel for estate planning, family business succession, and real estate matters in the Ligonier Valley.

Irwin, PA

We are just a short drive from Irwin and North Huntingdon. We help many Irwin-area clients with estate planning after major life events, like marriage or the birth of a child.

Export, PA

As our close neighbors in Export, we provide convenient and trusted legal services for real estate transactions and setting up new businesses.

Norwin (Irwin & North Huntingdon), PA

We are proud to serve the families and businesses in the Norwin area. Whether you're buying a home in North Huntingdon or starting a new business in Irwin, our team is just a short drive away.

Fox Chapel, PA

Our firm provides discreet and experienced legal counsel for Fox Chapel residents. We specialize in sophisticated estate planning, trusts, and real estate matters to protect your assets and legacy.

Wexford, PA

As one of the fastest-growing areas, Wexford is a hub for new families and businesses. We assist Wexford clients with everything from business formation and contracts to creating comprehensive estate plans.

Sewickley, PA

Ament Law Group offers experienced counsel for Sewickley residents, particularly in the areas of family business succession, high-net-worth estate planning, and complex real estate transactions.

Bethel Park, PA

Our services extend to the South Hills, including Bethel Park. We provide accessible legal help for real estate closings, probate administration, and setting up wills and trusts for Bethel Park families.

Our Commitment to You

No matter where you are in the region, our goal is to be your trusted legal partner. We combine local understanding with practical experience to get you the best possible outcome.
