About Ament Law Group

Experience. Trust. Community.

Our Firm's Philosophy

Trust is the foundation of every interaction we have with our clients, ensuring we represent them in the best manner possible. Clients trust us for many reasons, and we believe our significant and practical experience is key to earning that trust.

At Ament Law Group, P.C., we are committed to providing personalized and effective legal solutions to individuals and businesses throughout Western Pennsylvania. With over 50 years of combined experience, our attorneys specialize in estate planning and administration, real estate transactions, and business law. We pride ourselves on building lasting relationships based on trust, guiding clients through significant life decisions with clarity and confidence.

Our attorneys have successfully represented clients across various legal areas, including purchasing homes, establishing and transitioning businesses, closing businesses, drafting estate plans, avoiding probate, and administering estates. This diverse experience has enabled our attorneys to positively impact many aspects of our clients' lives and livelihoods.

Meet Our Team

Whether you're planning for the future, navigating a property transaction, or seeking business counsel, our team is here to support your goals with dedicated expertise. Our firm includes partners, associates, and of counsel attorneys who bring a wide range of specializations and decades of combined experience to every client relationship.
