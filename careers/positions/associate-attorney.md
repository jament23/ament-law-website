Our Murrysville-based law firm is seeking an ambitious Attorney with 3–5 years of experience to join our transaction-heavy practice. We don't just process files — we deliver peace of mind through sophisticated commercial and residential real estate, estate planning, and business services.

We are looking for a "mid-level" professional with "senior-level" drive — someone who is as comfortable drafting a complex trust as they are networking to build their own client base.

## The Opportunity

This isn't a "back-office" role. You will be a visible face of the firm, managing client relationships from consultation to closing. For the right candidate, this is a clear track to a Partner role within a dynamic, tech-forward environment.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Strategic Counsel:** Lead consultations for estate planning, administration, and business services, tailoring legal strategies to diverse client needs.
- **Precision Drafting:** Produce high-quality, legally sound documents for real estate transactions and business formations.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Proactively identify legal hurdles and provide preventative advice to safeguard client assets and legacies.
- **Business Development:** Actively market yourself and the firm to grow your personal book of business and firm-wide originations.
- **Tech Leadership:** Utilize Lackner, SoftPro, and Office 365 to maintain a seamless, efficient workflow.

## Who You Are

- **The Professional:** You hold a J.D. from an ABA-approved school and are licensed to practice law in Pennsylvania.
- **The Expert:** You have a proven track record in estate, business law, and/or real estate transactions. (Bonus points if you are a PA Title Insurance Agent or Notary.)
- **The Entrepreneur:** You have an "owner" mindset. You don't wait for direction — you see a need and fill it.
- **The Communicator:** You can translate "legalese" into actionable guidance with a calm, empathetic demeanor that puts clients at ease.

## Why Join Us?

- **Location:** Enjoy a sophisticated practice without the Downtown Pittsburgh commute.
- **Culture:** A collaborative, professional, and tech-adept environment that values meticulous work and ethical standards.
- **Compensation:** $55,000–$95,000 per year, commensurate with experience, plus rewards for originations. We value the business you bring in as much as the work you put out.
- **Comprehensive Benefits:** Health, Dental, Vision, and Life Insurance; 401(k) with matching; Paid Time Off; Professional Development assistance.
