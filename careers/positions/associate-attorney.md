Our Murrysville-based law firm is seeking an Attorney with 3–5 years of **substantive, verifiable experience** to join our transaction-heavy practice. We don't just process files — we deliver peace of mind through sophisticated commercial and residential real estate, estate planning, and business services.

We are looking for a candidate who can demonstrate — not just claim — real competency. You should be able to walk into a client meeting on day one, identify the legal issues, and execute the work product with minimal hand-holding. If your experience has been largely observational or limited to research and memo-writing, this is not the right fit.

## The Opportunity

This isn't a "back-office" role. You will be a visible face of the firm, managing client relationships from consultation to closing. For the right candidate, this is a clear track to a Partner role within a dynamic, tech-forward environment.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Strategic Counsel:** Lead consultations for estate planning, administration, and business services, tailoring legal strategies to diverse client needs.
- **Precision Drafting:** Independently produce high-quality wills, trusts, powers of attorney, deeds, business formation documents, and real estate transaction documents. We expect work product that is client-ready with minimal revision.
- **Real Estate Transactions:** Manage residential and commercial closings, including title review, document preparation, and settlement. Experience with SoftPro is a plus.
- **Estate Administration:** Guide personal representatives through the probate process, including preparation of Orphans' Court filings, inheritance tax returns, and accountings. Experience with Lackner is a plus.
- **Business Formation & Transactions:** Draft and review operating agreements, articles of incorporation/organization, buy-sell agreements, and related transactional documents. Experience with PA business entity filings is a plus.
- **Risk Mitigation:** Proactively identify legal hurdles and provide preventative advice to safeguard client assets and legacies.
- **Business Development:** Actively market yourself and the firm to grow your personal book of business and firm-wide originations.

## Required Qualifications

- **J.D. from an ABA-approved law school** and active license to practice law in Pennsylvania. Must be in good standing with the PA Disciplinary Board.
- **Minimum 3 years of direct, substantive experience** in estate planning, real estate, and/or business law. Candidates must be able to describe specific matters they have handled, the types of documents they have drafted, and the outcomes they have achieved.
- **Demonstrated ability to independently manage client matters** from intake through completion. We will ask you to describe your role on specific engagements — not your firm's role, but yours.
- **Proficiency in Pennsylvania-specific practice**, including familiarity with Title 20 (Probate, Estates, and Fiduciaries Code), the PA Rules of Professional Conduct, Orphans' Court procedures, and real estate settlement practices.
- **Strong analytical and drafting skills.** Attention to detail is non-negotiable. Errors in legal documents create liability for clients and the firm — we need someone who catches issues before they leave the office.
- **Professional demeanor** with the ability to communicate complex legal concepts clearly and empathetically to clients.

## Preferred Qualifications

- PA Title Insurance Agent license and/or Notary commission.
- Experience with Lackner (estate administration), SoftPro (real estate settlements), and/or Office 365 in a legal practice setting.
- Experience with business formation and transactional work, including entity structuring, commercial contracts, or buy-sell agreements.
- Existing professional network or book of business in Western Pennsylvania.
- Familiarity with local practice in Westmoreland, Allegheny, or surrounding counties.

## Why Join Us?

- **Location:** Enjoy a sophisticated practice without the Downtown Pittsburgh commute.
- **Culture:** A collaborative, professional, and tech-adept environment that values meticulous work and ethical standards.
- **Compensation:** Competitive salary commensurate with verified experience, plus rewards for originations. We value the business you bring in as much as the work you put out.
- **Comprehensive Benefits:** Health, Dental, Vision, and Life Insurance; 401(k) with matching; Paid Time Off; Professional Development assistance.
