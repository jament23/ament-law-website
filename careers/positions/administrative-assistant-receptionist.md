Our Murrysville-based firm is growing quickly, and we are looking for a high-energy, detail-oriented Administrative Assistant/Receptionist to join our team. With over over a century of combined legal experience across Westmoreland and surrounding counties, we pride ourselves on being the trusted voice for families and businesses.

We need a professional who can juggle a busy multi-line phone system one minute and assist an attorney with sensitive estate documents the next — all while maintaining a calm, welcoming presence for our clients.

## The Role

This is a full-time, in-person position where you will act as the central hub of our office operations. You aren't just "answering phones" — you are managing the client experience from the moment they walk through the door until their legal matter is successfully closed.

## Key Responsibilities

- **Client Coordination:** Act as the primary point of contact — greeting visitors, managing multi-line phones, and coordinating complex calendars.
- **Legal Support:** Attend client meetings to take detailed notes and collaborate with attorneys to ensure all action items are captured.
- **Document Management:** Prepare petitions, legal forms, and general correspondence with a high degree of accuracy.
- **Execution & Follow-Up:** Assist in formal document execution meetings and proactively manage post-meeting tasks to keep files moving forward.
- **Office Flow:** Maintain an organized, professional environment and handle administrative duties as required to support our growth.

## Qualifications

- **Experience:** 2–4 years of experience in a legal office setting (experience in Real Estate, Business, or Estate Law is a significant plus).
- **Tech-Savvy:** High proficiency in Microsoft Office 365 and the ability to learn legal-specific software quickly.
- **The "Soft Skills":** A friendly, empathetic demeanor with "five-star" customer service instincts.
- **Reliability:** A self-starter who thrives under pressure and takes pride in meticulous organization.
- **Local:** Must be able to commute reliably to our Murrysville office (15668).

## Benefits & Compensation

- **Competitive Salary:** Commensurate with your legal experience.
- **Comprehensive Benefits:** Health, Dental, Vision, and Life Insurance.
- **Financial Future:** 401(k) and Retirement Plan options.
- **Work-Life Balance:** Paid Time Off (PTO) and a consistent Monday–Friday, 8-hour day shift.
