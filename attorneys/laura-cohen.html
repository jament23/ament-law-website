Biography

Laura Cohen, Esquire is Of Counsel with Ament Law Group and focuses her practice on estate planning and estate administration. She brings more than three decades of experience and is one of the few women attorneys in the Eastern Suburbs who concentrates her legal work in estate law.

Attorney Cohen is known for her compassionate approach, ethical standards, and deep commitment to helping families navigate complex legal matters with clarity and confidence.

Before joining Ament Law Group, Attorney Cohen founded Family Legal Center, LLC in 1996 with the goal of offering personalized, community-based legal services. She has served clients primarily in Allegheny and Westmoreland Counties and has earned recognition throughout her career for both her legal ability and her professionalism.
